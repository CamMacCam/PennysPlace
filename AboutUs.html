Our Mission

Penny's Place specializes in professional dog grooming and spa treatments in the Aylmer area of Gatineau. We offer customized services for all breeds and coat lengths, ensuring a relaxing experience for your dog. Penny's Place is a young, aiming to expand its services across the Outaouais region. We focus on transforming your dog from scruffy to clean, providing expert grooming services along with relaxing spa treatments.

Cameron and Penny - Owner and inspriration

Penny is the heart and soul of our business and the inspiration behind everything we do. She’s an energetic one-year-old Bernadoodle who wasn’t always a fan of bath time. Everything changed when we started pampering her with soothing oils, massages, and a whole lot of care. Seeing how much she began to enjoy her bath time, we were inspired to create an experience that every dog could love.
